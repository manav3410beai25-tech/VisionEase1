Shipping & Delivery
Global

Yes, we offer global shipping across all continents via secure express delivery routes. Orders are processed within 24 hours and typically arrive within 5–10 business days internationally.

Delivery

Domestic orders take 2–4 business days. International orders take 5–10 business days. Express overnight shipping is available at checkout for an additional fee.

Insurance

All orders above $150 are automatically insured during transit. For orders below this threshold, optional shipping insurance can be added at checkout for $4.99.

Products & Safety
Medical

Absolutely. All our smart lenses are rigorously tested under medical scrutiny and are CE & FDA approved for daily wear up to 16 hours. Each batch undergoes clinical trials before market release.

Custom

Yes. All spectacle frames can be fitted with custom prescription lenses. Simply upload your prescription at checkout, and our opticians will prepare your order within 3–5 business days.

Materials

Our frames are crafted from aerospace-grade titanium, plant-derived acetate, and carbon fibre composites — lightweight, hypoallergenic, and built to last a decade of heavy daily use.

Returns & Refunds
Policy

Implanted bionic devices are final sale due to strict sanitary and medical protocols. However, external spectacles and non-implanted smart lenses carry a standard 30-day return policy, no questions asked.

Returns

Email us at returns@visionease.com with your order number. We'll send a prepaid shipping label within 24 hours. Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days of receiving the item.

Damage

We're sorry that happened. Take a photo of the damage and email it to support@visionease.com within 48 hours of delivery. We'll ship a replacement free of charge via express delivery immediately.

Still have questions?

Our team is available 24/7 — we usually respond in under 2 hours.
